# 🎲 DM REFERENCE — *The Gilt Conspiracy*
> CONFIDENTIAL — For DM use only. Do not share with player.

---

## 🌍 SETTING
**Varenholm** — a mid-sized city built around the Varenholm Arcane Academy, which has trained mages for 300 years. Magic is respected but regulated. Commoner mages are rare and often looked down upon by old-money alumni families.

---

## 🕵️ THE CONSPIRACY (Full Truth)

**Chancellor Marius Vael** made a secret pact with **Solveth the Unraveler**, a dying god of entropy, roughly 12 years ago. The deal:
- Marius receives **immortality** (he is actually 140 years old — his aged appearance is a glamour he maintains to seem approachable)
- Solveth receives **magical essence** to sustain himself and eventually resurrect fully

**The method:** Every graduate's **Assignment Seal** is subtly corrupted. Rather than simply marking the mage's specialty and assignment, it acts as a **slow siphon** — drawing raw magical essence from the mage over months/years and funneling it into **The Hollow Crown**, a relic buried in the lowest sub-basement of the Academy (a level no student has ever accessed).

**The endgame:** Once The Hollow Crown reaches full saturation (roughly 3–4 months from the current graduation ceremony), Solveth can use it to physically manifest, burning out all living siphon-bearers (i.e., any mage who received a corrupted seal) in the process. This includes all current graduates AND any living alumni from the last 12 years.

**Why hasn't anyone noticed?**
- The essence drain is gradual — mages just feel slightly less sharp over time, chalked up to stress
- Several mages who got too close to the truth have been quietly reassigned to remote postings or had "accidents"
- Marius controls assignment postings, records, and has influence over the city council

**The player's seal burned differently** because the player is a Blade Channeler with unusually raw, unrefined charisma-based magic — the seal "tasted" it and drew more than expected on first contact. This means the player's siphon is faster than others, but also means Solveth is faintly aware of the player specifically.

## 👥 KEY CHARACTERS

### 🎓 Chancellor Marius Vael
- **Role:** Primary antagonist
- **Appearance:** Elderly, silver-bearded, warm eyes — a deliberate glamour
- **True age:** ~140 years old
- **Motivation:** Genuine fear of death, not malice. Believes what he's doing is a "necessary sacrifice for the greater good of magical progress." He tells himself the mages live mostly normal lives.
- **Knows about the player?** After the ceremony, yes — the seal flared unusually and Marius noticed. He's watching.
- **Weakness:** He genuinely loved the Academy once. That love can be used against him.
- **End scenario options:** Can be defeated, redeemed (if player learns his backstory), or sacrificed to seal Solveth permanently.

### 📖 Instructor Elara Ash
- **Role:** Reluctant ally
- **Appearance:** Late 30s, sharp-featured, always has ink-stained fingers
- **What she knows:** She noticed the pattern — graduates from the last 12 years are subtly declining magically. She's been quietly pulling records. She doesn't know about Solveth yet, only that the seals are wrong.
- **Why she hasn't acted:** She has no proof and Marius is beloved. She's also afraid.
- **Trigger to open up:** If the player approaches her with genuine concern after the ceremony, she'll cautiously begin sharing pieces. She becomes a full ally after Lyra's seal accelerates.
- **Secret:** She received her own seal 8 years ago as a graduate. She is being slowly drained too and doesn't know it.

### ⚔️ Lyra Voss
- **Role:** Best friend, first visible victim, emotional stakes
- **Appearance:** Dark-haired, sharp-eyed, quick wit, commoner background like the player
- **What happens to her:** Because she and the player are assigned to the same village, they're together when her siphon accelerates (triggered by a magical event in Duskfen that causes a surge). She begins losing her spellcasting ability faster than she should. She won't admit something is wrong at first.
- **Her arc:** Denial → fear → anger at the Academy → becomes a fierce ally once the truth is out
- **Do NOT kill her** unless the player makes a choice that leads there. She should survive as the emotional reward.

### ⛪ Brother Donn
- **Role:** Information broker / unexpected ally
- **Appearance:** Portly, kind-faced city priest of a minor healing deity. Seems unremarkable.
- **What he knows:** Over the past 3 years he's quietly documented 14 young mages who came to him with vague fatigue, magical sluggishness, and spiritual unease. He has a private ledger. He doesn't know the cause but suspects the Academy.
- **Where to find him:** The Temple of the Wandering Flame, two streets from the Academy gate. He can be found after the player returns from Duskfen.
- **Role in plot:** Gives the player the first hard evidence — the ledger of affected mages — and the name of one who vanished: **Tomas Carver**, a graduate from 7 years ago who was about to go public.

### 👁️ Solveth the Unraveler
- **Role:** True antagonist / morally complex final encounter
- **Nature:** A dying god of entropy and endings. Not evil in the traditional sense — he is the divine concept of *things coming apart*, which has its place in the cosmic order. He is desperate and frightened, which has made him complicit in something monstrous.
- **How he communicates:** Through the player's dreams, starting after the first night in Duskfen. He will not lie outright but will be manipulative, framing himself as a victim.
- **What he wants:** To be heard. To survive. Or, if the player can offer another way, to end peacefully.
- **Resolution options:**
  1. Destroy the Hollow Crown — Solveth dissipates, Marius loses immortality and ages rapidly (dies or survives as a broken old man depending on player choice)
  2. Redirect the Crown — If the player can get a willing soul to offer their essence freely (not siphoned), Solveth can be sustained peacefully without harm. Elara may volunteer.
  3. Marius sacrifices himself — if redeemed, Marius can offer his own accumulated stolen essence back to Solveth as a final gift, dying in the process and freeing all siphon-bearers.

## 📍 LOCATIONS

### Varenholm Arcane Academy
- Grand old institution, 7 towers, vast library, underground vaults
- Sub-basement Level 3 (restricted): Contains The Hollow Crown chamber
- Marius's private office: Contains his hidden journal (key evidence) behind a false bookshelf
- The Sealing Chamber: Where graduation ceremonies are held — the corruption device is built into the podium

### Village of Duskfen
- 3 days east of Varenholm
- Small farming community near a marshland
- The "creature sightings" are real: unraveling spirits (minor manifestations of Solveth's entropy leaking through weak planar points near the village's old mill)
- The mill sits over a ley line that Solveth has been using as a secondary drain point
- Resolving this is the player's first assignment — but it will also be the first clue something bigger is happening

### The Temple of the Wandering Flame
- Brother Donn's home base
- Contains his private ledger
- Has a warded back room where sensitive conversations can be had safely

### The Hollow Crown Chamber
- Final dungeon
- Guarded by entropy constructs (beings of pure dissolution)
- The Crown itself is a blackened iron circlet covered in runes — beautiful and deeply wrong
- Destroying it requires either overwhelming magical force or the right ritual (Elara knows it, if she's alive and trusted)

## 📅 PLOT STRUCTURE (Acts)

### ACT 1 — Graduation & Departure
- Graduation ceremony / seal branding (DONE in opening scene)
- Player notices something off (the burn, Elara's look)
- Player and Lyra depart for Duskfen
- **Player decision point:** Investigate now or head out?

### ACT 2 — Duskfen
- Arrival in Duskfen, meet locals
- Investigate the creature sightings at the old mill
- Discover the unraveling spirits and the ley line drain
- Lyra's seal begins accelerating — she starts losing spells
- First dream from Solveth (cryptic, unsettling)
- Player resolves the mill situation (combat/puzzle/negotiation)
- Return to Varenholm with more questions than answers

### ACT 3 — The Investigation
- Elara approaches the player (or player approaches her)
- Meet Brother Donn, get the ledger
- Begin sneaking into Academy restricted areas
- Find Marius's journal — the full truth is revealed
- Marius realizes the player knows — sends an assassin (a corrupted graduate turned construct)
- Confrontation / escape

### ACT 4 — The Reckoning
- Race to the Hollow Crown chamber before it reaches full saturation
- Navigate the sub-basement (puzzles, traps, constructs)
- Encounter Solveth directly (dialogue-heavy)
- Final decision: destroy, redirect, or redeem
- Epilogue based on choices made

## ⚠️ DM REMINDERS
- Let the player discover things organically — don't feed the conspiracy
- Solveth's dreams should feel sympathetic, not villainous
- Lyra's decline should be gradual and heartbreaking, not sudden
- Marius should feel genuinely warm and likeable until the reveal — the horror is that he's not a monster who pretends to be good, he's a good man who became monstrous
- Always give the player at least 3 meaningful choices at decision points
- Track which characters the player has met and what they know
