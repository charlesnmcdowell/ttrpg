# The Sundered Gate — Chapter Ten: The Pipeline

**Day Eighteen — Afternoon**

---

Deeper east. Channels run together — then the ground opens its mouth.

Crater. Two hundred yards. Carved. Bottom's a compound: stone, black glass, angles that hate curves. Red glow behind the windows. Three gates in a triangle — all humming.

Varn: "That's not a relay."

"That's a house," Finch says. "With neighbors."

Thessaly: "Someone lives down there. On purpose."

Senna doesn't debate. Two fingers — Finch left, Varn right — and they're moving. Gold-team clean: close the gates, don't give the house time to wake up.

---

End of The Sundered Gate — Chapter Ten.
