# The Sundered Gate — Chapter Twelve: The Workshop

**Day Eighteen — Afternoon**

---

Deeper. Workshop: benches, crystals, half-built constructs. Map table — whole Bleakmoor in red ink, gates, relays, siphons.

Pinned note: *Phase 3: Convergence unsealed by outside actor. Accelerate timeline. Route energy to primary basin. Open the Sundered Gate before the ArchMagus finds it.* Two days old.

I read it aloud.

Thessaly: "So we're not chasing an accident. We're chasing a schedule."

Glass Stalker drops from the ceiling — eight limbs, wall-colored until it isn't, vector straight at Thessaly.

Her staff catches a reflection that shouldn't exist. Crystal Beam up — glass cracks — it becomes *visible*.

Cyclone. Cyclone. Second hits the seam. Detonation. Limbs in eight directions.

She's still at the map table. Spectacles clean. "You're welcome."

She reads. I pace. Names surface — Mordecai Corven, ex-Academy, ley architecture. Sundered Gate: sealed before the Academy, twelve miles northeast, fed by every relay in the net. Money through a comm array — supplies, materials, constructs.

"He didn't break the moor," she says. "He's renting it."

---

End of The Sundered Gate — Chapter Twelve.
