# The Sundered Gate — Chapter Eleven: The Threshold

**Day Eighteen — Afternoon**

---

Thessaly and I descend sixty feet of carved glass. Compound door stands open — not kicked, **invited**. Ward line on the threshold: alarm, not blade.

"They want a visitor log," I say.

"They want witnesses," Thessaly says. "Or bait."

Corridor: black glass, red glow, symbols that aren't Academy — older geometry, same handwriting as the relays.

The hum isn't gears. It's lungs through stone.

Junction. Two Void Weavers — abyssal casters, strip-and-dodge types.

Horizon Arc down the hall. Both in the sweep. Three Cyclone throws, back to back. Fire, frost, dust. They're dead before they finish deciding we're real.

Thessaly lowers her staff. "I had a plan."

"You can use it on the next two."

---

End of The Sundered Gate — Chapter Eleven.
