# The Sundered Gate — Chapter Twelve: The Iron Coliseum

## DING — END OF CHAPTER TWELVE

Five rounds in my underwear. The Lord of Ironholt bleeding from the face, frost in his shoulder, his best trick turned to colored glass on his belt. Eight thousand people screaming at a man who solved an axe problem by getting undressed.

The ember sits different after that fight. Not stronger. Not deeper. Wider. Something about reading Dren's enchantments — the whole architecture laid bare in two seconds of God Sight, every layer and shortcut and one-pass-too-few mistake visible like looking at a blueprint. The enchantment reading isn't new. The SPEED of it is. Two seconds to map ten years of a genius's layered work. That's not Level 18. That's something the ember is doing on its own now. Growing into the space that keeps opening.

The constructs are part of it. Sorcerer's Hegemony — the perk that came with 18. The portals aren't just doors anymore. They're garrisons. Twenty-four constructs spawning per day across six gateways. Gold-black glass. Faceless. Entropy-veined. I used them against Greave like chess pieces and I sent one to Elara carrying my wardrobe like a valet. The network is an empire and the empire builds its own army whether I'm watching or not.

I need to check the fear reports. Six portals. Two squads each after two days. Forty-eight constructs standing guard in five settlements and a crater. Varenholm. Duskfen. Broken Antler. Mines. Thornwall. Bleakmoor. Villagers don't sleep well near things that look like the abyssal threat they're being protected from. The Council will add this to their file. Another line. Another reason.

Haste is gone. That hurts. The concentration check on Veritas's second swing — CON save, the one weakness in the build, and Dren found it in two hits. INT 20 doesn't need to know the mechanic. He felt the speed drop and filed it.

He wants to talk after. The grin he left the ring with wasn't a loser's grin. It was a recruitment grin. The Lord of Ironholt found someone who fights like an engineer and he's not going to let that walk away without a conversation.

Senna at 261 HP. Scaling after every fight. Varn came in with six vials brewed specifically for her — Nullroot hit, suppressed her healing for a full round — and it wasn't enough. She healed him in the ring after. She's 100% poison immune now from his vials. But the cold and necrotic resistances from our spar — those are three days old. Long rests purge them. When we fight in the semifinal, Frost Fang hits her at full strength. She doesn't know that yet.

Sera is in the ring. Rapier only. No Shard. No magic. Fighting the Counterspell specialist with steel because I told her what she already knew.

Semifinal bracket: Kenji versus Senna. Second time. She's stronger. But she bleeds to cold and necrotic now. The math changed and she doesn't know it changed.

36,000 EXP from the Dren fight. 64,400 from Bleakmoor ops via Hearts and Minds. Total this chapter: 100,535. Running total: 397,770. Level 19 threshold is in the mirror. Next long rest, the ember expands again.

453 gold. Noble's Interest plus Golden Age income. By the time the Sundered Gate hits critical, the ArchMagus has more gold than the Academy's annual budget. The Council will notice.

The gate is at 56%. Eight percent per day. Five days.

End of The Sundered Gate — Chapter twelve.
