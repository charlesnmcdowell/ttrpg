# The Sundered Gate — Chapter Fourteen: Tournament's Eve

**Day Eighteen — Night**

---

Thornwall at night. Iron Coliseum behind the registration office — stone and iron built for violence at a scale that makes the Bleakmoor feel honest.

Two guards watch me step from a fifteen-foot entropy-lined portal. One mutters, "Wrong door for a picnic."

"Next time I'll bring a basket."

Emberfrost on my back. Moor dust on my boots. The ember hums like it's already read the bracket.

Tomorrow: forms. Day after: blood.

---

End of The Sundered Gate — Chapter Fourteen.
