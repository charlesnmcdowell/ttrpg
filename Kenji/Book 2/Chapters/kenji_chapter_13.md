# The Sundered Gate — Chapter Thirteen: Orders

**Day Eighteen — Dusk**

---

Senna closes three gates. Wardbreakers on the crater rim — dust, sweat, nobody smiling like it's a party.

"Compound clear?"

"Looted for paper," Thessaly says. "Mordecai Corven. Sundered Gate twelve miles northeast. He's not a storm — he's a contractor."

Finch: "Great. I hate landlords."

Varn: "We leaving breadcrumbs?"

"Elara gets the journals," I say. "Garrett gets the short version so he doesn't torch the Draft."

Senna's eyes flick to me. "You?"

"Tournament registration. If I'm going to bleed in public, I want a number."

She nods once. Orders settle: Thessaly — Varenholm. Senna — Garrett. Me — paperwork and a queue.

Bleakmoor Ruins portal gutters out. New gate at the crater lip — fifteen feet, gold-green entropy thread. Portal six rebuilt; the net stops screaming.

Senna goes through last. Look back — not warmth, not threat. Just *confirmed*.

---

End of The Sundered Gate — Chapter Thirteen.
