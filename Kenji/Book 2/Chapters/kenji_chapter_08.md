# The Sundered Gate — Chapter Eight: The Eggs Are Fine

**Day Eighteen — Morning**

---

Dawn. Silver Draft. My own ceiling above me for the first time in three nights.

Level 15. Three levels overnight like a detonation. The Bleakmoor did what the siphon used to do, in reverse — fed the current off a hostile ley that had no idea I was running through its teeth. Stronger. Faster. A sixth register trying on shoes somewhere behind my sternum.

Solveth, through the pillow: *You slept like a dead man.*

"Feels like it."

*Don't test anything large today until after breakfast.*

"Noted."

---

Academy. Aldwin has two drills waiting on the workbench — a metronome and a throwing-weight — and the tired expression of a man who has already run this lesson in his head four times.

"Haste first. One slip in focus and you're human again, which in your weight class is worse than it sounds. Giant's Throw second — steel comes home angry. If you throw it without the recall locked in, it will come back through whatever is in the way."

My hands blur when Haste holds. The world doesn't slow; *I* speed up until physics starts filing complaints. I throw Frost Fang three times at the far wall. It comes back on the third try without leaving a divot.

Aldwin grunts. The closest he gets to praise.

---

Silver Draft, Hour 9. Senna is on her third plate. Auburn braid. Eyes haven't looked up once since I walked in.

Per Pip, she arrived at dawn, walked in without a word, sat down at the bar, pointed at a menu, and has not stopped eating since. The reconstructed order of events is: Kenji portals her from Bleakmoor rim while she's mid-punch. She lands sixty miles deeper into the Bleakmoor than she expected to be. She runs the sixty back in the dark. Kills four things that get in her way. Finds a note pinned under a rock in Finch's handwriting — *Gone to Varenholm. Use the portal. —F. P.S. He says you can use his portals now.* She uses the portal. She walks into the inn. She orders eggs.

"You threw me through a portal."

"How was your jog?"

The fork bends in her fist.

"Sixty. Miles."

The bent fork goes on the table. She picks up a new one from the next setting without looking.

"Your friend behind the bar told me you charmed my arcanist, recruited my scout, and threw me sixty miles so you could build infrastructure while I was unconscious."

"Well I figured I couldn't beat you by force. You know, with the healing factor, the teleporting, the hard-hitting fists. So I opted for the next best thing. How's the eggs, by the way? This inn has the best eggs."

"The eggs are fine."

From the corner, Varn: "She's had four plates."

"The eggs are FINE, Varn."

---

End of The Sundered Gate — Chapter Eight.
