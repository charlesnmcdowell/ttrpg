# Fraying Empire — Chapter 16: *Listening Roof Left Behind*

**Session span:** Ashmere 30 (post–guest-chamber **long rest** through same evening) → **Ashmere 31** dawn (Day 252–253).  
**Corridor:** March **guest chamber** (wake, same calendar day as Ch15 end) → **inner cordon** (burned circle, **Frost Fang** at hip, threshold work) → **negotiation** → **egress** → south road **northbound** → **Thornkeep** gates → **Thornkeep Waystation** hired room → **long rest**.

---

### Guest chamber — Ashmere 30, after the latch

He woke with **full sheets** and **full slots** — Chapter 15’s exhale — and the room still behaved like a **witness**. Same **Ashmere 30**; same **listening roof**; no day-skip yet. Hunger and pride argued quietly until hunger won enough to move his feet.

### Burned circle and blade truth

Deeper cordon work stayed **physical** before it stayed **political**: **Frost Fang** carried at the hip (not the nodachi layout), lintel marks read like old homework, **Solveth** audible at the wrong moments — the kind of truth a border keeps in its teeth. He crossed a **burned floor circle** with her in **human form** once — **hands joined** on the ritual stone — not rim theater; **inside** the geometry, where the March measures willingness.

### Bargain, bluntness, and a bronze calling card

After Chapter 15’s **listening roof**, the day still refused to stay tidy: **contract language** offered and **deflected**; a **mask-name** offered under pressure — **Seren** (pretty, spendable, **not** the gated true name in `character_tracker.md`) — then the ronin did what low **INT** often does under pressure — he tried to **flatten court into appetite** and, in the same breath, **fed the house a map** (Thornkeep inn, academy strings, **Kenji** on the wind).

He left a **bronze trophy** in her palm anyway — a sympathetic token in a place where sympathy is **inventory**.

The roots did not argue. They **filed**.

### Permission without mercy

The March did not chase him out with spears. It **opened** — corridors remembering his feet, green pulse dimming where pride would have sharpened it.

She did not say *stop*.

What she gave instead was the kind of permission creditors give: **go**, because the ledger already owned something worth more than his shoes.

### North until the world smells like oats again

Mist thinned to grey sky and honest gravel. Tabards returned — distant at first, then real: **Thornkeep**, slate and lamp smoke, the Coalition’s habit of pretending stone cannot lie.

He paid for a room at **Thornkeep Waystation** (Coalition-contract travel house — the kind of roof that files strangers into categories without inventing drama). Key, latch, window facing a street honest about **tabards**. **Bag of Holding** where it belonged. Habits first, confession never.

### Vigil

He waited for a knock that meant **her**, or **them**, or **anyone who knew his name**.

Footsteps passed. Laughter downstairs. A runner’s boots — urgent, **not for him**.

**Nobody asked for Kenji.**

When the boards went quiet enough to pretend the world had forgotten him, he ate **March lean rations** because bodies do not negotiate with drama.

Sleep arrived easier than it should have — which was its own warning, and he took the rest anyway.

### Dawn ledger

**Ashmere 31**, Thornkeep inn — **long rest** complete: slots and charges as refreshed at table; **HP** full; the hired roof did not answer back.

**Three gold** for the night. The room empty in the way an empty room can still feel **watched** from miles south.
