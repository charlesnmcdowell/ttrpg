**Session span:** Ashmere 29 dawn through Ashmere 30 dawn (Day 251–252).  
**Corridor:** East verge mile ~17 → south road crown (solo) → Pallid March **forward cordon** (root-crown amphitheater, circulation heart, guest chamber).

### Split after the copse

**Ashmere 29** opened on **Rootday** where Chapter 14 left it: birch copse **~mile 17**, charges fresh, **Oathbreaker** in the **Bag of Holding**, **Corwyn** still a living question north on the wheel ruts.

**Kenji** ordered the split he had already been building in his head: **Jostin** and **Brynn** north — Thornkeep, **Bracken**, the Coalition paper spine, safety that could be explained to captains and councils. **Kenji** south — not because courage is a monologue, but because the March’s command vector would not come to him if he stayed polite beside other people’s fires.

Watch-fire geometry stayed as **language**: east ring, crown sightline, proof the corridor remembered discipline even when the ronin walked away from it.

### Crown march

Plain miles. No **Wind Step** burn unless the road decided to argue. The mist pretended indifference; the dead zone’s old logic still listened south.

Terminal: a **root-crown** court that did not read like a camp. It read like a **heart** somebody had taught to beat in formation.

### The Lady’s table

A thrall stepped out of the mist — face too calm — and set a tray on a root-shelf that should not have been a table. **Kenji** ate anyway: not fast, not polite; the way a man eats when pride stops negotiating with hunger.

The woman on the root-throne watched him the way a cat watches a bird that refuses to fly.

When he **sat** — back straight, **nodachi** still within honest reach without performing threat — the room changed what it showed him. Warmth that stopped convincing. A thread of wrong light under the cheekbone — teal ink trying to remember it was blood once.

She spoke low. **Poison** was not the test. **Fear** was.

**“Keep looking,”** she invited — because most men fight, kneel, or try to charm a person they think they can win.

He had simply **sat down**.

### Rations and cadence

Kenji asked for **bulk rations** for travel. She permitted thralls to stage lean march weight — scaled in play toward roughly **three weeks** of preserved stores into **satchel / Bag of Holding** staging — enough to mock his appetite and respect it at once.

**Irresistible Presence** did not take the night off. **DC 23**, **female humanoids**, **viewing** on the **15-minute cadence** with cumulative **−1** to the save per window (see `kenji_tracking_OBSOLETE.md`). A **living attendant** burned through saves and climbed hard on the **stack ladder** (procreative urge, lingering stacks with fade timers once viewing breaks). **The Lady** in **human form** fought the same clock with a mind like a fortress — and still **lost ground** across long exposure: deep band pushing **ravenous / overwhelming** territory by the time steam replaced stone.

### Baths — “equals”

Kenji asked for a **bath**. She named the price of courtesy — then he narrowed the room: **no thralls**, only her; **he** would wash **her**, then she him — **as equals**.

She laughed the way age laughs at a pretty lie — and accepted the theater.

**Hand taken** like a gentleman, **bow**, follow into steam and stone. He stripped weapon and clothes, went to his **knees**, head **bowed** until she entered the water — then looked up when commanded.

**“I’m Kenji.”** A plain name in the wrong house.

He framed a bargain while oil met skin: **two demands** from each while he served and cleaned — equals language she corrected without letting go of the game.

Her first demand: **no lies he believes** — omission allowed; performance not.  
Her second: when she asks **who his enemies are** — **names**, or **silence** — not poetry.

His demands were begun — **“simple”** — and never finished clean before the scene turned.

### Water and withholding

What followed was **touch** without crossing the line her biology treats as execution: **not intercourse** — therefore **Close to Death never triggered** (see `character_tracker.md` — Lady Nyx). Still: proximity, legs over legs, washing shoulders to waist, the **stare** feeding the aura both ways she could not pretend away.

When he **stopped**, **stood**, and looked down — inspection without charity — she stayed composed where pride mattered and **honest** where biology betrayed her.

Then he **grinned** like a man borrowing D&D language in hell: tired, **long rest**, **“mam,”** a room.

She granted it — **warm**, **dry**, a latch that locks inside — and named the truth of palaces: a latch that **listens** outside.
