## 1. Arc compass (what this arc *is*)

This is **Threat 2 — Pallid March** at knife range: not the full continental clock (that lives in `fraying_empire_campaign.md`), but the **immediate southern corridor** where the Lych tests borders, harvests conscripts, and runs a **necromantic root network** as battlefield C2.

**Kenji’s declared posture (Book 4 Ronin):** He is burning cover slowly. Full ember / portals / signature ArchMagus tells identify him; the March *loves* creation-flavored targets. So the arc stresses **iaido + nodachi + smoke clone + senses**, with ember held for “mask already off” moments.

**The arc’s job in the larger campaign:**

- Deny or degrade **forward observation and column cohesion** so the Coalition gains time.
- Learn **how** she commands (roots, revenants, formation discipline) without treating every fight as the final boss fight.
- Keep **Sir Corwyn the Fallen** (death knight, Lawful Good binding twisted toward *her*) as a recurring pressure: proof her bindings work on heroes, and a future hinge (break binding vs kill).

**“Done” for this *push* (pick one when satisfied):**

- A) **Military exit:** Party reaches a defined safe line (e.g. Thornkeep debrief, Bracken’s map updated, Coalition message dispatched) with casualties/intel logged.
- B) **Intel exit:** Kenji confirms what the **living observer** is (scout? bound thrall? third party?), what the root pulse **south** is feeding, and whether the wall’s adjustment (formation closed the seam) presages a **larger maneuver** or a **delay pattern**.
- C) **Boss tease exit:** First direct contact with a **death knight or revenant commander voice** that redefines stakes — then cut to long rest / travel beat so you do not stall in endless skirmish.

**Not required to “close” this file:** killing Lady Nyx, breaking the bronze ring, or stopping Still Night — those are **campaign-tier** resolutions.
