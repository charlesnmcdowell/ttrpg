# The Iron Crown War — Chapter One: Come

## Day 30 — Morning

---

The Confluence Lens fires at dawn. Seven anchor points. Three days of work compressed into two. Silver light condenses into a beam that punches through clouds and bends sixty miles southwest.

The Colossus moves. Expansion, not ascension. The volcanic stone fills the tear from inside. The ley network heals.

Mordecai drops to his knees. Hands on cracked stone. "It worked."

Thessaly checks her instruments three times. "The tear is closed. Completely. As if it never happened."

The Sundered Gate is gone. Permanently.

---

## Day 30-32 — Silence

---

The whisper stone goes dead. Three days. No signal. No static. Nothing.

Day 31 — nothing.

Day 32 — nothing. God Sight reads the eastern horizon from Mordecai Ridge. The ley lines are wrong. Bent. Compressed. Something heavy sitting on them.

---

## Day 33 — Dawn

---

The whisper stone crackles. Kex's telepathic signal bleeds through first — walls, fire, shapes on the horizon. A drake screaming across a link being crushed.

One word from Dren. Flat.

**"Come."**

---

The war starts in an afternoon.

Vess gets the brief. The sharp eyes don't flinch at eight thousand. She pulls deployment charts and turns an oversight office into a war room in ten minutes.

Seventy constructs march through the Ironholt Approach portal. Sera and Vael deploy with eight fighters. Senna takes her Wardbreakers behind enemy lines. Brindle and Kael hit supply routes.

The ArchMagus handles the siege tower personally. Three crits in four throws. Emberfrost at two hundred yards. The tower falls in six seconds.

Dren looks up from a dead soldier. Sees the man standing in the wreckage. Purple dreadlocks. Twin blades. The ember burning visible through enchanted leather.

"About time."

---

Six properties purchased in Crestfall. An eighty-person civil service hired. The Wizard King's empire grows even while the Wizard King fights a war.

Garrett takes the news without expression. "You're building a government. A boring, paper-pushing, ink-stained army. And it's going to be the most dangerous one you have."

---

Elara reads the ley disturbance from her crystal array. "Someone built a machine that eats ley lines. And they parked it next to Ironholt."

She deploys east with Senna's team. The apparatus must be found.

---

## Day 33 — Afternoon

---

Stormhaven. Southern coast. Twelve warships in the harbor. Two thousand soldiers in dark red armor. Blade Prince Drazen's occupation — clean, methodical, a prince proving himself.

Carrick Hale is in the streets. Stocky. Adaptive armor. A silver cat on his shoulder. Evacuating civilians through a Vyranthos patrol.

"You're not Vyranthos."

"Sup."

Duchess: "I like him already."

The portal goes up in an alley. The conversation turns to Five Guys and cajun fries. Two men from Earth standing in a fantasy war zone talking about cheeseburgers. Five years of loneliness cracking open in both directions.

Then Kenji rises above the harbor.

Duality. Creation Mode. Haste. Giant's Throw. Emberfrost.

Sixty seconds. Twelve warships. The blockade ceases to exist.

Sixty-five minutes. Eighteen hundred soldiers. The occupation ceases to exist.

Carrick watches from a rooftop. Duchess on his shoulder.

"He killed eighteen hundred soldiers in sixty-five minutes. By himself."

"Charles is processing. Give him a moment."

---

Aldwin agrees to teach Greater Invisibility. His price isn't gold. It's a future.

"War College Dean. You're building an empire. Empires need officers. I train them."

---

Thessaly walks toward the Deepwood with four construct squads and a Veylan staff that the elves built. The only human the Confederacy might listen to.

Torvald's trackers shadow Mordecai. The gentleman's understanding holds. But shadows have been added to the margins.

---

Day 33. One day. One man.

A siege tower destroyed. A harbor sunk. An army deleted. An empire administered. A spell commissioned. Allies deployed across a hundred miles of territory that didn't exist on any map a week ago.

The Iron Crown War has begun. The Wizard King is awake.

---

*End of The Iron Crown War — Chapter One: Come.*
